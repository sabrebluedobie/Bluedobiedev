About Ken's Trailer Sales

Around the beginning of the COVID-19 pandemic, a start-up trailer sales company was poised to make a big difference in the trailer and side-by-side market in rural southern Missouri. This area would cover the states of Arkansas, Kentucky, Missouri, and Tennessee.

In an area famous for fun outdoor activities such as hunting, fishing, camping, and watersports, this was a natural entry point. Many people in this area are no strangers to hard outdoor work, and use their trailer in the pursuit of their business activities. This business had hoped to sell Rice Trailers as well as some side-by-side outdoor vehicles.

However, the COVID-19 pandemic was in full swing when the business plan was ready to go. Due to the unsure nature of the economy at that time, the business was unable to get up and running.

About Devin

Devin is a smart, hard-working young man who has some big ideas. He is not afraid to roll up his sleeves and get the job done. His beautiful, spunky daughter is the inspiration for the name and the logo for the company. I thank Devin for allowing me to be part of this project.

In creating this site, I built the site from the ground up using HTML and CSS languages. I developed a unique custom logo for the business branding, a color scheme based on Devin's trademark color pattern and custom trailer icon for use on the browser tab.
