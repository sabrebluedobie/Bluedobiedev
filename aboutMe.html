About me


My name is Melanie Brown, and I am proud to welcome you to my portfolio page. I have had a full life as a wife, a mother, and a grandmother. I love going on adventures with my husband and our two dogs. My dog, Sabre Blue, a Doberman Pinscher, was the inspiration for #sabrebluedobie. I wanted to make something fun and interesting by building this business. By doing this, I am showing that I am ready to find work that I am passionate about.

Enjoying the Work

I love to code and watch projects transform on-screen based on what I enter. I enjoy the challenges associated with the work, as I enjoy solving problems. I look forward to sharing my work with you!
